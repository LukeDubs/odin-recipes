Bramble

The Bramble is essentially a gin sour, spiked with an eye-catching shot of blackberry liqueur.


Ingredients

  • 50ml gin
  • I 25ml lemon juice
  • ¾tbsp sugar syrup
  • Ice cubes
  • Crushed ice
  • ¾tbsp crème de mure
  • 1 lemon slice and a blackberry to serve (optional)

Method

  1. Shake the gin, lemon juice and sugar syrup in a cocktail shaker with a good handful of ice cubes
  2. Strain into a rocks glass full of crushed ice.
  3. Drizzle the crème de mure over the top so it 'bleeds' into the drink.
  4. Garnish with the lemon slice and blackberry, if using.
